Skills vs CGPA: What Matters More in Engineering?

Most first-year students get stuck between two extremes: either they chase CGPA only, or they ignore it completely.

CGPA helps early

It can help with eligibility and shortlisting in some situations.

Skills decide long-term career

Skills help you get better work, internships, and confidence.

The balanced approach

Maintain a decent CGPA while improving skills slowly. You don’t need to rush.
