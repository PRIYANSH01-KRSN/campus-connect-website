Is CGPA Really Important in Engineering?

CGPA matters, but it’s not everything. The real problem is students either over-fear it or ignore it completely.

Where CGPA matters

  • Shortlisting for some internships
  • Higher studies eligibility
  • First job screening (sometimes)

Where CGPA matters less

  • Skill-based roles
  • Long-term growth
  • Real-world performance

The best approach

Aim for a decent CGPA, but don’t destroy your mental health. Build skills alongside academics.
