How to Manage Time in Engineering College Without Stress

Time management advice online often feels unrealistic. The goal is not perfection — the goal is consistency.

1) Don’t over-schedule

If you plan every minute, you’ll fail quickly. Leave breathing space.

2) Prioritize daily

Every morning ask: what matters most today? Do that first.

3) Consistency beats motivation

Small focused daily action is better than random bursts.
